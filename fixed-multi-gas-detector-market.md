<p><p>The Fixed Multi Gas Detector market includes several technologies for detecting hazardous gases. Catalytic combustion sensors are ideal for flammable gases, utilizing combustion to measure concentration. Electrochemical sensors (echem) offer high sensitivity for toxic gases through chemical reactions. Infrared (IR) detectors excel in measuring gases like CO2 and CH4 by detecting their unique light absorption characteristics. Photoionization detectors (PID) are effective for volatile organic compounds (VOCs), employing UV light to ionize gas molecules and indicate concentration levels.</p></p>
