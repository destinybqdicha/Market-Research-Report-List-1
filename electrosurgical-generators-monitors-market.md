<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrosurgical Generators Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Vessel Sealing Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrosurgical generators monitors market includes various types, primarily classified into monopole, bipolar, and vessel sealing generators. Monopole electrosurgical generators utilize a single active electrode to transmit energy for cutting or coagulating tissue, requiring a grounding pad. Bipolar electrosurgical generators use two electrodes, allowing for precise energy transfer directly to the tissue without a grounding pad, ideal for delicate procedures. Vessel sealing generators specifically focus on sealing blood vessels using high-frequency energy, providing reliable hemostasis during surgeries.</p></p>
