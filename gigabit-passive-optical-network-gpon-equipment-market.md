<p><strong>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network GPON Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) equipment market comprises three primary components. The Optical Line Terminal (OLT) serves as the central hub, managing data traffic between the service provider and end-users. Optical Network Terminals (ONTs) are situated at the customer's premises, converting optical signals into electrical data for devices. Passive Optical Splitters distribute the optical signal from the OLT to multiple ONTs, enabling efficient bandwidth sharing without requiring active components, thus enhancing network efficiency and reducing costs.</p></p>
