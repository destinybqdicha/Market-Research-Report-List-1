<p><p>Thin wafer processing involves slicing semiconductor wafers into thinner sections to improve efficiency and performance in electronic devices. Different dicing equipment are used for this purpose: blade dicing utilizes a diamond-coated blade to mechanically cut wafers, ensuring precise and smooth edges. Laser dicing employs high-energy lasers for non-contact cutting, offering flexibility and reduced mechanical stress. Plasma dicing uses ionized gas to etch micro-vias or channels, ideal for delicate materials. Each method has unique advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
