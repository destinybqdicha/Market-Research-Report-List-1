<p><p>The sintered metal fiber felt market consists of three primary types: stainless steel fiber, iron fiber, and aluminum fiber. Stainless steel fiber felt is known for its corrosion resistance and high-temperature stability, making it suitable for industrial filtration and heat exchange applications. Iron fiber felt, typically more cost-effective, is used in less critical applications due to its susceptibility to rust. Aluminum fiber felt is lightweight and offers excellent thermal conductivity, ideal for aerospace and automotive sectors, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
