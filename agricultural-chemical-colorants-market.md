<p><p>The agricultural chemical colorants market encompasses two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants that are often used in formulations for herbicides, insecticides, and fertilizers, providing vibrant colors for identification and safety. In contrast, pigments are insoluble particles that impart color without dissolving, ensuring stability and longevity in various agricultural applications. Both types enhance product visibility, aid in branding, and facilitate easier handling and application, benefiting the overall agricultural industry through improved safety and marketing.</p></p>
