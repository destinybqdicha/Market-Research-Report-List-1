<p><p>Fluorescence immunoassay analyzers are essential tools in diagnostics, employing different methods for detecting specific antigens or antibodies. Enzyme-linked fluorescent immunoassays utilize enzyme-substrate reactions to produce a fluorescent signal. Chemiluminescence immunoassays rely on light-emitting chemical reactions. Radioimmunoassays use radioactive isotopes to measure the concentration of substances. Other types may include multiplex assays or alternative detection methods, expanding the flexibility and applications in clinical and research settings. Each type offers unique advantages in sensitivity and specificity.</p></p>
