<p><p>Damper actuators are essential components in HVAC systems that control airflow through ducts. Spring return damper actuators utilize a spring mechanism to automatically return the damper to a predetermined position when power is cut, ensuring safety and energy efficiency. In contrast, non-spring return damper actuators maintain their position even when power is lost, allowing for more flexibility in control. The market for these actuators evolves with advancements in automation and increasing demand for energy-efficient climate control solutions.</p></p>
