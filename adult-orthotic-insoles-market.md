<p><p>The adult orthotic insoles market is segmented into two main types: half palm and full palm. Half palm insoles provide support mainly under the arch and heel, offering targeted relief for conditions like plantar fasciitis or general foot pain while allowing flexibility. In contrast, full palm insoles cover the entire footbed, delivering comprehensive support and alignment from heel to toe, which is beneficial for those with more severe foot issues or require additional stability. Each type caters to different needs and preferences.</p></p>
