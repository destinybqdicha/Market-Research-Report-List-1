<p><p>The Low Power Single Frequency Solid State Laser market is categorized into two primary types: Pulse Type and Continuous Wave (CW) Type. Pulse Type lasers emit energy in short, high-intensity bursts, making them suitable for applications like precision machining and medical procedures. In contrast, Continuous Wave (CW) lasers provide a steady output of light, ideal for applications requiring constant energy, such as spectroscopy and telecommunications. Each type serves distinct industries, driven by their unique operational characteristics and advantages.</p></p>
