<p><p>The Semiconductor and Electronics X-Ray Inspection System market includes two primary types: 2D and 3D X-ray inspection. 2D X-ray inspection provides a flat image, effectively detecting surface defects and connectivity issues in electronic components. In contrast, 3D X-ray inspection offers volumetric imaging, allowing for more detailed analysis of complex assemblies and hidden internal structures. Both technologies enhance quality control and reliability in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to different inspection needs based on component complexity and inspection depth.</p></p>
